Blog #5

Link: https://www.gamedeveloper.com/design/combating-toxicity-in-online-games#close-modal

Toxicity in games is a major problem, and it will continue growing as more and more people are being exposed to the internet. Toxicity in games, simply put, is conveying your negative emotions towards someone else online; a form of cyberbullying. It is important to understand and put an end to because it will create a better environment for people to learn and enjoy. Some ways to combat toxicity in games include:

  • Rewarding good behavior, giving incentive to become a behaved player.
  • Discouraging bad behavior by communications banning, que banning, and chat banning.
  • Using AI, it can auto-detect explicit words/hateful words, banning the offender on the spot.
