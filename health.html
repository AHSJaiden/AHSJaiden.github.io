Fitness Trackers 
Health Technology
 

A fitness tracker is a device that tracks your movement, such as a traditional pedometer or other wearable device, or a smartphone app.

Features:  

-Gradually increases daily step count 

-Holds user accountable for daily exercise

-Gives incentive to workout 

-Helps with Heart Health  

Evidence:

"In the mActive study, a randomized clinical trial, Martin and his Johns Hopkins colleagues tested an automated, real-time, personalized program that sent coaching text messages to cardiology patients based on data from a wearable device paired with their phone. Over the short term, this coaching system helped increase step counts by more than a mile a day beyond tracking alone."

-Hopkins Medicine
